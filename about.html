About

Cakes with a hint of creativity and loads of freshness are what define The Cake Shop. With unique flavors that blend perfectly with each other, this place will never fail to surprise you. Not only are they masters in cake baking,The Cake Shop also has a wide range of cupcakes to spoil you! What puts them apart is that they also bake bread for their own sandwiches which is heavenly! With fresh cakes, assorted cupcakes, brownies, and a side of coffee, this is not a place to be missed!
